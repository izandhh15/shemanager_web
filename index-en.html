Everything You Need

The full football manager engine, built for women's football.

Live Match Engine

Experience every match in real time. Make substitutions, change formation and adjust team mentality on the fly.

International Transfers

Sign players from all 5 top women's leagues. Negotiate, loan, or recruit free agents from across Europe.

Youth Academy

New prospects every season. Develop tomorrow's stars of women's football from the grassroots up.

11 Competitions

Liga F, Copa de la Reina, Supercopa, UWCL, Europa Cup, 4 foreign leagues and the Women's World Cup 2027.

World Cup 2027

176 national teams with real confederation qualifying systems. Final at the Maracanã, August 2027.

Realistic Economy

Budgets calibrated to real women's football. Every euro matters more than in the men's game.

Open Source · Free

No payments, no microtransactions, no tricks. The code is open. SheManager is the first open-source women's football manager, based on Virtua FC by Pablo Román.
